About

I was born and raised in Yuba City, a small town that rests at the foot of the Sutter Buttes. My childhood involved many camping, hunting and fishing trips throughout California. This sparked my undying passion for exploring the outdoors and capturing its beauty in my artwork.

After high school, I moved to San Luis Obispo and earned my degree in Business Information Systems at Cal Poly. Since graduating, I have furthered my education by learning computer programming and started my career as a web developer. If you need a website or perhaps you have an exisiting one that needs a mobile-friendly design then follow the links to contact me below.
