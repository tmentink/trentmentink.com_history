About

I was born and raised in Yuba City, a small town that rests at the foot of the Sutter Buttes. My childhood involved many camping, hunting and fishing trips throughout California. This sparked my undying passion for exploring the outdoors and capturing its beauty in my artwork.

After high school, I moved to San Luis Obispo and earned my degree in Business Information Systems at Cal Poly. Since graduating, I have furthered my education by learning computer programming and started my career as a web developer.

Health

Outside of my passion for art, I am also a bit of a health enthusiast. In my final years of college, I began putting a greater emphasis on my health. It started with learning more about nutrition and has evolved into a strong commitment to being a little bit better than I was the day before.

I firmly believe that investing in one's health will lead to greater self-confidence, increased happiness and the ability to achieve one's goals. While I don't claim to know it all, I can share with you what has worked for me. If you are interested in learning how to create a personalized diet plan then click on the link below.
