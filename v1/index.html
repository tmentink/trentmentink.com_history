About

Trent was born and raised in Yuba City, a small town that rests at the foot of the Sutter Buttes. His childhood involved many camping, hunting and fishing trips throughout California. This sparked his undying passion for exploring the outdoors and capturing its beauty in his artwork.



After high school, Trent moved to San Luis Obispo and earned his degree in Business Information Systems at Cal Poly. Since graduating college, Trent has furthered his education by learning computer programming with aspirations of becoming a web designer.
